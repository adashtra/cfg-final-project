TRAVELLING SAFELY DURING COVID-19

TRAVELLING BY PLANE

  • Wear a face covering on the flight and in the airport
  • Wash your hands regularly, particularly after touching surfaces
  • Carry your own hand sanitiser with you
  • Practice social distancing from people outside your household
  • Minimise the time you spend near other people

WHEN YOU ARRIVE

Travellers vaccinated against COVID-19 or recovered from it:
  • You will not need to quarantine
  • You must show a certificate of vaccination/previous Covid infection

Travellers not vaccinated against COVID-19 and not previously infected:
  • There is a mandatory quarantine of 5-6 days from your arrival
  • If both tests are negative, your quarantine will end
  • You will need to take 2 PCR tests, one when you arrive, and another on the 5th day

DURING YOUR TRIP

  • Regularly monitor the news and the Icelandic government website for changes to Covid restrictions
  • From June 26th 2021:
    • There are no restrictions on gatherings
    • Masks are no longer mandatory
    • There are no rules for social distancing
    • You will need to quarantine for up to 14 days if you contract Covid-19 or come into contact with someone who is infected
