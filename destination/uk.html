TRAVELLING SAFELY DURING COVID-19

BEFORE YOU GO

  • If you’re travelling to a different nation within the UK, make sure to read the rules for that nation
  • Be aware of rules regarding the number of people allowed to holiday together
  • Stock up on face coverings and hand sanitiser to take with you
  • Download the NHS Covid-19 app to easily check in to locations that require track and trace information

DURING YOUR TRIP

  • Follow the Covid regulations for the UK nation you are holidaying in
  • Regularly monitor the news and UK government website for any changes to restrictions
  • If you develop any symptoms of Covid-19, get tested as soon as possible
  • Minimise contact and observe social distancing with others outside of who you’re travelling with
  • Be prepared to self-isolate for up to ten days if you contract Covid-19 or come into contact with someone who is infected
